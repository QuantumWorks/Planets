1: Install and configure the PocketEssentials. 
2: Make a mine world with ores and make a bedrock spawn. 
3: Make a preset planet world and set spawn. 
4: Copy Planet.php to {PocketMine}/plugins/PMEssPlugins/ folder. 
5: Copy your two worlds folder to {PocketMine} folder and makre sure they are in PocketMine world format. 
6: Edit Planet.php in your PMEssPlugins folder and change the world name. 
7: Put signs on your server:
